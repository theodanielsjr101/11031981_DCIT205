Admission to our computer science program is competitive, and we seek students with a strong academic background and a passion for exploring the diverse and dynamic field of computer science.

Our computer science program offers a comprehensive curriculum designed to provide students with a strong foundation in both theoretical concepts and practical skills. Core courses cover fundamental topics such as algorithms, data structures, programming languages, and computer organization. Additionally, students have the opportunity to explore specialized areas through elective courses, including artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, software engineering, and more.
